Transformer Oil Filtration – Core Components & Process

Key Components of the Transformer Oil Filtration Machine

Each unit is built around a controlled process that removes gases, moisture, and contaminants from transformer oil, restoring safe insulating and cooling properties.

  • Degassing chamber – strips dissolved gases and moisture from the oil under vacuum.
  • Vacuum pump – creates the deep vacuum required for efficient moisture and gas removal.
  • Oil heater – preheats oil to reduce viscosity for better filtration and dehydration.
  • Filtration system – high-efficiency filters remove sludge, carbon, and solid particles.
  • Condensers – condense extracted water vapour and gases for safe collection and disposal.
  • Control panel – monitors temperature, pressure, flow and vacuum; automates and protects machine operation.

Working Principle – Step-by-Step

The machine uses controlled heating, vacuum dehydration, degassing, and fine filtration to deliver clean, reliable transformer oil back to service.

  • Heating – oil is preheated to a controlled temperature to lower viscosity and support moisture removal.
  • Degassing & dehydration – in the vacuum chamber, dissolved gases and water are vaporised and extracted.
  • Fine filtration – oil passes through multi-stage filters that capture sludge, dirt, and other solid contaminants.
  • Cooling & recirculation – clean oil is cooled (if required) and returned to the transformer or storage tank.

Transformer Oil Filtration Method

iEngineering supplies systems suitable for routine maintenance as well as intensive treatment of heavily contaminated transformer oil.

  • Single-stage filtration – for periodic maintenance and oil with minor contamination.
  • Double-stage filtration – deep purification of oil with high moisture and gas content.
  • Mobile / trolley units – skid or trailer-mounted systems for on-site field use.
  • On-line filtration systems – continuous purification while the transformer remains energised (where application allows).
  • Heating & centrifugation options – for additional separation of sludge and free water where required.

Types of Transformer Oil Filtration Machines

iEngineering can supply transformer oil filtration systems tailored to different duty levels and site conditions.
