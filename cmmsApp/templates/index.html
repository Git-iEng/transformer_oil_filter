Technology

Smart Water Management – Core Components

Smart Water Management Solution – Metering Technology

Smart meters provide frequent and accurate data 24/7 using wireless communication. Readings are uploaded to the processing server for analysis, billing, alarms, and reports.

  • Ultrasonic / Electromagnetic meters for high accuracy.
  • Digital comms preferred over 4–20 mA for precision and diagnostics.
  • IEC 60529, IP68 options; explosion-proof designs available.
  • Built-in batteries keep meters operating during power failures.

Loggers / Converters

Gateways and RTUs capture meter pulses/data and forward via 3G/4G/GPRS or NB-IoT for central processing. Data is reliable, secure, and timestamped for audits.

  • RS-485/Modbus support for multi-drop networks.
  • Battery powered field units for remote sites.
  • Totalizer logs, reverse-flow detection, and pressure/quality sensors.

Software Integration

iEngineering connects smart water data to EAM/CMMS and SCADA for work orders, alarms, dashboards, and analytics. Managers and technicians can monitor quality, quantity, and pressure—anytime, anywhere.

  • REST / MQTT / Modbus TCP bridges.
  • Role-based dashboards, reports, and maps.
  • Event-driven work orders in CMMS from leak/exception alerts.

Battery Powered Sub-Metering & Network Topology

Battery Powered Sub-Metering Solution

Collected data is processed via a 3G/4G/GPRS interface (BCT). One BCT connects up to 16 RMTs and supports up to 200 water meters, enabling wireless end-to-end integration for real-time monitoring and analysis.

  • Reliable performance and scalable architecture.
  • Built-in batteries keep meters and loggers running during outages.
  • Suitable for utilities and industrial campuses.

Sub-Metering Network

GRM (GPRS Transmission) connects up to 32 RMTs within ~1 km. Each RMT (RS-485/Modbus) links up to 40 meters within ~150 m.

  • One GRM can collect data from up to 247 meters.
  • Optimised transmission costs with hierarchical aggregation.
  • Suits dense precincts and multi-building sites.
